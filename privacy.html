Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2025

1. Who we are

TrustRebuild (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides educational resources to help couples rebuild trust.

2. What information we collect

  • Contact Data – name, email address, WhatsApp or Telegram number (optional).
  • Usage Data – pages visited, links clicked, form interactions (via analytics cookies).
  • Communications – messages you send us by email or chat.

3. How we use your information

  • To email or message you the free trust‑building guide you requested.
  • To send relationship‑support updates (only if you gave consent via the checkbox).
  • To improve and secure our website.

4. Legal basis

We rely on your consent (GDPR Art 6 (1)(a)) and our legitimate interest to operate this site (GDPR Art 6 (1)(f)).

5. Data sharing

We never sell your data. We share it only with:

  • Email service providers (e.g. Mailchimp) to deliver the guide.
  • Form processors (e.g. Formspree/Getform) to securely collect submissions.
  • Analytics services (e.g. Google Analytics) – anonymised usage data only.

6. Retention

Contact data is kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Analytics data is stored for 26 months.

7. Your rights

You may access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and withdraw consent at any time. Email us at support@example.com.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site security and optional analytics cookies (banner notice shown on first visit).

9. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and stored with ISO 27001‑certified providers.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; we’ll post the new version with a revised date.

11. Contact

Questions? Email support@example.com.
